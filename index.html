DABChain is a network of trust.

In our world of ever changing wireless technology and rapidly developed electronic devices, a non-traditional system of accounting for authorized devices, independent of intermediary parties is necessary.
‍
The Device Authorization Blockchain decentralizes the current system of recording, tracking, and maintaining the list of authorized devices in the market and provides trust through verification by the node operators of the network.

This mitigates the risk of trusting a single entity while reducing the costs of relying on third party. By using blockchain technology, a system of trust by verification is created.
Decentralized

The consensus mechanism is used validate data and there is no need for intermediaries to perform actions.

Immutable

All entries into the system are immutable which means once the data is written it cannot be changed.

Reliable

Network operates without any possibility of downtime, censorship, fraud, or third-party interference.

Secure

All events on the blockchain are cryptographically secured verified by node operators.

Transparent

Updates are tracked and permanently recorded on a distributed and public ledger.

Fast

Blockchain transactions are automatic and no manual verification is necessary.

Technology that is fault-tolerant.

Designed for the modern era

Data is distributed to many devices connected to a network of nodes with no possible single point of failure.

Network based infrastructure

Robust system infrastructure and network that is both convenient to use and tamper-proof.

A system with infinite uptime.

The DABChain platform provides immutable records of certified devices and their technical information, all securely stored on the blockchain and independent of any centralized entity or third-party.
